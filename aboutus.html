Who We Are

Hi! We’re RentoMojo, an online rental solution for all your furnishing and lifestyle needs. Founded in 2014, we understood that buying expensive furniture and appliances isn't always the smartest choice – especially in a fast-moving urban life.

We provide high-quality furniture, appliances, electronics, fitness gear, and even two-wheelers on flexible monthly rentals. No heavy upfront costs, no maintenance worries – just pay for what you use and upgrade anytime.

With free delivery, installation, maintenance, and relocation services, we make renting simple, affordable, and convenient across major cities in India.
