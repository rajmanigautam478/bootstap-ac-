How the Referral Program Works
  1. You refer a friend using your unique referral code/link.
  2. Your friend rents from us for the first time using your code.
  3. Upon successful delivery and completion of the order, you earn RentoMoney!
Important Terms & Conditions

These are the terms and conditions for the referral program offered by Rentomojo, operated by Rentomojo Private Limited (formerly Edunetwork Pvt. Ltd.). The program offers RentoMoney as a sign-up bonus for the referrer and a bonus for the friend when the friend signs up for the first time using the referrer's unique code, and their rental order is successfully delivered.

  • The friend must be a first-time customer using the referrer's unique code for their first rental order on Rentomojo.
  • To qualify for the referral bonus, the monthly rental must be INR 500 or higher (excluding security deposit).
  • RentoMoney is applicable on monthly rental payments only.
  • Upon successful order completion and delivery, referrers receive Rs 400 in RentoMoney (amounts may vary as per current offer).
  • Only registered users can utilize RentoMoney.
  • The referral code cannot be combined with other available offers and discounts during the checkout process in certain cases.
  • Referrers cannot use the code of someone they have referred.
  • If the friend cancels their subscription before delivery, the referral bonus is cancelled for both parties.
  • The maximum referral bonus for a referrer is capped and may vary depending on the referral offer.
  • RentoMoney is non-transferable and cannot be converted into cash upon completion of the rental term.
  • RentoMoney remains valid for one year from the date it is credited.
  • Adding funds to the RentoMoney account is exclusively through referral bonuses.
  • Rentomojo holds the right to remove referral codes posted on social media and Google Play Store.
  • Rentomojo Pvt. Ltd. has the authority to withdraw or cancel RentoMoney in case of fraudulent activity.
